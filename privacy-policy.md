# Privacy Policy for Together

**Effective date:** March 12, 2026

Together ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Together mobile application ("App") handles user and device data.

## 1. Data We Process

Together is designed to work primarily with local on-device storage.

Depending on how you use the App, the following data may be stored on your device:

- Relationship start date
- Special dates and events (such as anniversaries and birthdays)
- Partner information you enter
- Memories and photos you add
- Notification preferences
- App language preference

## 2. Permissions We Use

The App may request the following permissions:

- **Photos/Media (optional):** Used only when you choose photos for memories.
- **Notifications (optional):** Used to send reminders for your saved special dates.

You can deny or revoke these permissions in your device settings.

## 3. Data Sharing and Selling

- We do **not** sell your personal data.
- We do **not** share your personal data with third parties for advertising.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored locally on your device.
- We recommend using device security features (such as screen lock) to protect your data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Together is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the Effective date.
